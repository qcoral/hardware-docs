Here's a list of useful resources to help you build your projects

## Finding 3D models:

- [GrabCAD community library](https://grabcad.com/library) (this has LITERALLY any 3D model you can think of)

## Project inspiration:

- [Adafruit] is one of my favourite resources! There are literally hundreds of insanely cool projects out there

## Communities

### Discords:

- [KiCAD](https://www.kicad.org/community/chat/) (PCB design and help)
- [QMK](https://discord.com/invite/qmk) (Keyboard firmware)
- [VORON](https://discord.com/invite/voron) (3D printing help)

### Subreddits:

- [r/mechanicalkeyboards](https://reddit.com/r/mechanicalkeyboards) (keyboard help)

## Measurements list:

These are the measurements I use to design parts for 3D prints!

- M3 screw head diamter: 6mm (this is both for socket head and button head - they're approx. the same)
- M3 SHCS screw head diameter:
- M3 heatset inserts: 4.7mm OD
- M3 screw-ins for plastic: 2.9mm
- M2 screw-ins for plastic: 1.9mm
